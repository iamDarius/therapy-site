About Me

A Licensed Clinical Social Worker who's here to help.


Do you feel like you are struggling in your life and are looking for different ways to handle things? Do you feel stuck and feel it is impossible to change current behaviors? It can be hard to get unstuck in your life and see the changes that need to be made. I believe in the resiliency of people and the ability to change. I provide my clients with a safe nonjudgmental environment to explore difficult feelings, thoughts, and difficult past situations. My passion is working with people to help them find success, peace, and happiness in their lives. I am a strengths-based therapist who has advanced training in trauma treatment working with adults, adolescents and children.

I have been providing counseling/therapy for over 15 years and been an LCSW (Licensed Clinical Social Worker) since 2009. I have been providing treatment for sexual behavior problems for the last 13 years and was Certified as a SOTP (Certified Sex Offender Treatment Provider) in 2007.

I am trained in the use of EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization Reprocessing) and Neurofeedback. I also provide supervision for licensure and CSOTP Certification training and supervision.


My Philosophy

My goal is to create change within the therapeutic setting for individuals that seek therapy. Using sound, ethical, and culturally competent services I provide therapy to individuals who seek to make changes in their lives. I implement a variety of tools, techniques, and interventions for those seeking change. I strive to meet each individual where they are in the therapeutic process, conduct a thorough assessment, and collaborate with these individuals to develop a plan for making the changes they want to see.
